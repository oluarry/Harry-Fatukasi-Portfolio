Newcomer App
This past semester we have accumulated lots of data while undergoing the design process in the face of our users' problems. We were
challenged with the task of helping immigrants, refugees and newcomers feel more welcome in Waterloo. To increase the complexity of this topic
we had to consider all of the ways that COVID-19 has impacted our society, school environment, the way we form connections and the way in
which we learn. It is already difficult being a newcomer in an unfamiliar environment and evidently, the pandemic has disconnected many
individuals, making it no easier. As our environment changes it is necessary to adapt to the adjusted needs of users, and innovate new solutions.
